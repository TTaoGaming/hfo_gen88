When an artifact survives the "Scream" of the Red Queen and matches the Inscriptions on the Shield, the Pyre Praetorian performs the **Rite of Resurrection**:
1. **Immersion**: The artifact is validated against the Shield Book.
2. **Rebirth**: The artifact is moved from `bronze/` to `silver/`.
3. **Sealing**: A Silver Receipt is issued, and the event is logged to the Blood Book of Grudges (as a clearance) and the Shield Book (as a success).
